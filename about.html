Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe

Home Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup softened butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F.
  2. Cream together the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar.
  3. Mix in the eggs and vanilla until smooth.
  4. In a separate bowl, combine the flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt.
  5. Slowly add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture.
  6. Stir in the chocolate chips.
  7. Scoop dough onto a baking sheet.
  8. Bake for 10–12 minutes or until golden brown.

Tips for Better Cookies

  • Use room‑temperature butter for better texture.
  • Chill the dough for thicker cookies.
  • Don’t overmix — it makes cookies tough.
  • Let cookies cool before eating so they set properly.
